Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TrustKenya and how we verify businesses.

TrustKenya is a comprehensive business verification and review platform designed specifically for the Kenyan market. We use advanced AI technology to verify businesses and authenticate reviews, ensuring you make informed decisions when choosing where to spend your money.

Our platform allows you to search for businesses by name, registration number, or till number, view their trust scores, and read authentic reviews from real customers. We also provide document verification services to help businesses establish their credibility.

We use a multi-layered verification approach:

  • AI-Powered Analysis: Our machine learning algorithms analyze business documents, receipts, and reviews for authenticity with 95% accuracy.
  • OCR Technology: We automatically extract and verify information from business registration documents and certificates.
  • Receipt Verification: Users can upload receipts to prove they've interacted with the business before leaving a review.
  • Fraud Detection: Our system monitors for suspicious patterns and fake reviews in real-time.

Yes! TrustKenya is completely free for consumers. You can search for businesses, read reviews, and write your own reviews without any charges.

For businesses looking to verify their company and access premium features like analytics, enhanced profiles, and priority support, we offer affordable subscription plans tailored to different business sizes.

Our Trust Score is a comprehensive metric based on multiple factors:

  • Verification Status: Whether the business has submitted and verified official documents
  • Review Quality: Number and authenticity of customer reviews
  • Response Rate: How quickly businesses respond to customer inquiries and complaints
  • Business History: How long the business has been operating and verified on our platform
  • Compliance: Adherence to Kenyan business regulations and licensing requirements

Scores range from 0-100%, with businesses graded from A+ (90-100%) to F (below 40%).

Absolutely! We take review authenticity very seriously. Every review goes through our AI-powered fraud detection system that checks for:

  • Verified transactions (users can upload receipts as proof)
  • Suspicious patterns like duplicate content or coordinated posting
  • Bot activity and fake accounts
  • Conflicts of interest (business owners can't review their own businesses)

Reviews marked with a "Verified Purchase" badge have been confirmed through receipt verification, giving you even more confidence in their authenticity.

Verifying your business is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Create a business account and complete your profile
  2. Upload required documents (Business Registration Certificate, KRA PIN, Trading License)
  3. Our AI system will verify your documents within 24-48 hours
  4. Once approved, you'll receive a verified badge and can start managing your profile

Verified businesses get increased visibility, the ability to respond to reviews, access to analytics, and build customer trust through transparency.
